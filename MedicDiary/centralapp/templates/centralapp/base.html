What is a health record

My Health Record lets you control your health information securely, in one place.
This means your important health information is available when and where it’s needed, including in an emergency.

Start using your My Health Record:

  • Log in to your record for the first time
  • Control your privacy settings
  • Understand what information can go in your record
  • Discuss My Health Record with your healthcare provider
