How to work with health records at MedicDiary

Register

The first step when beginning to use MedicDiary as a healthcare professional is to create an account at our website. While registering you will need to provide some educational and professional details which might be required for authentication purposes. Once this step is completed, you can go on to manage your patients' records.


Enter the access code

In order to access the health record of any of your patients, you are required to enter a verification cum access code which is unique to every patient. On successful verification of the access code, you will be given access to edit the health records of your patient(s).
You can view and manage all your patients under the "My Patients" section.


Maintain your profile as a healthcare professional

Under the "My Profile" section, you can edit and maintain your profile, add new qualifications or delete some previously entered data. Additionally you can also specify your availability timings and the corresponding hospitals.


Security and Privacy

At MedicDiary, we place a lot of trust in our healthcare providers, which is eventually essential for the right diagnosis and for the proper maintainence of a health record. Alongside, we also expect the healthcare providers to follow a general code of conduct for the maintainence of records available at MedicDiary, infringement of which may lead to cancellation of your membership at MedicDiary.
